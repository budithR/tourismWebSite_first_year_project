Sri Lankan Tourism

Sri Lanka is an island in South Asia. It is located in the Indian Ocean to the southwest of the Bay of Bengal and to the southeast of the Arabian Sea. So the sea is all around this island. Because of those calm and beautiful beaches, Sri Lanka has lots of tourist attractions. Not only the beaches but also beautiful and historical mountain ranges such as Knuckles and Hanthana increased the tourist attraction. So tourism is the main source of income in Sri Lanka. Srilankans, on the other hand, enjoy traveling throughout the country. And another interesting thing is camping. Both locals and tourists like to camp in Sri Lanka.

Camping

Camping is a very interesting, exciting, and adventurous activity. Mainly in Sri Lanka, there are a few camping types such as beach camping, camping on a mountain, and camping in a jungle. Ussangoda, Panama, Mirissa, and Balapitiya are famous for beach camping, and Knuckles, Narangala, Lakegala, Kabaragala, and Idalgashinna are famous mountains for camping.
